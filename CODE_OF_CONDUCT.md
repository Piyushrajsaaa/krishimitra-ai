# Code of Conduct - KrishiMitra AI

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in KrishiMitra AI harassment-free
for everyone regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender,
level of experience, nationality, race, religion, or background.

We are especially committed to being inclusive of:
- Rural farmers with low digital literacy
- Non-English speakers especially Hindi speakers
- Contributors from all Indian states and regions
- Agricultural domain experts without coding background

## Positive Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for farmers
- Show empathy towards community members
- Help new contributors get started
- Write code comments in simple English
- Provide Hindi translations where possible

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others private information
- Dismissing non-technical domain experts
- Making fun of low-resource or rural contexts
- Any form of discrimination

## Enforcement

Report violations to: krishimitra.ai@gmail.com

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

### Enforcement Guidelines

1. Correction - Minor behavior - Private written warning
2. Warning - Single violation - Warning with consequences
3. Temporary Ban - Serious violation - Temporary ban
4. Permanent Ban - Pattern of violations - Permanent ban

This Code of Conduct is adapted from Contributor Covenant v2.1
